Our Real Estate Breakthrough

In 2023, KASSOSA Investment Partners Club achieved a significant milestone with the acquisition of our first commercial property in the growing suburb of Kasawo. This strategic investment represents the culmination of five years of disciplined saving, careful planning, and unwavering commitment from our members.

The property, a one acre commercial land, was acquired for 50 million Ugandan shillings and has already shown promising returns through rental income from three established businesses. This project demonstrates our club's ability to identify valuable opportunities and transform collective resources into tangible assets that benefit all members.

From Vision to Reality: The Journey

The journey began in 2020 when our founding members recognized the need for a structured investment vehicle that would allow Kasawo Secondary School alumni to pool resources and build wealth together. Through monthly contributions ranging from 100,000 to 500,000 shillings per member, we gradually built our investment capital while conducting thorough market research.

"This project isn't just about bricks and mortarâ€”it's about building a legacy for future generations of Kasawo alumni. It proves what we can achieve when we unite behind a common vision." - Mr. Batasi Herbert, Chair Person of KASSASO

After eighteen months of diligent saving, we formed a property selection committee that evaluated over fifteen potential investments before identifying our current property. The location was chosen for its proximity to the newly constructed Kasawo Town Center and its potential for appreciation as infrastructure development continues in the area.

Strategic Value and Member Benefits

Beyond the financial returns, this project serves multiple strategic purposes for our club. It provides a physical asset that anchors our investment portfolio, generates steady rental income that can be reinvested, and demonstrates our credibility to potential partners and financial institutions.

For our members, the project offers both tangible and intangible benefits. Financially, it represents a secure investment with projected annual returns of 12-15%. Perhaps more importantly, it strengthens the bonds between alumni and creates a powerful example of what collaborative economics can achieve within our community.
